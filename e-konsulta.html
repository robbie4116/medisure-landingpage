A.I.D.A. Your AI Diagnostic Assistant

What is A.I.D.A?

A.I.D.A is a tool to help clinicians provide better over-all patient care by managing patient information through concise summaries of relevant details needed during patient visits. It helps in better clinician efficiency by reducing manual documentation and improved patient-doctor engagement.

The Need in the Philippines

In the Philippines where physician to patient ratio is 1:28,000 (WHO standard is 1:1000), A.I.D.A can assist physicians, especially in underserved areas, through easy access of patient's medical and his or her family history, including laboratory and imaging results as stored in its electronic health record.

Data Correlation

A.I.D.A correlates all stored patient data and processes initial findings for better insights.

Efficiency Boost

Reduces manual documentation and improves patient-doctor engagement significantly.

Physician Verified

All findings are subject to confirmation by a licensed medical practitioner.

Important Note

A.I.D.A. as a digital tool is not meant to replace diagnosis by licensed medical practitioner but solely to assist in improving over-all patient care management.

Eligibility

To avail of A.I.D.A, a user must be a licensed medical practitioner in the Philippines and is accredited by Medisureonline.
