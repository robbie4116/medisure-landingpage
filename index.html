Our Story

How MediSure Plus came to life and our mission for accessible healthcare

March 2020

The Beginning: A Crisis Becomes an Opportunity

The story of MediSure Plus started in March 2020 when Metro Manila was declared on lockdown due to the pandemic. A group of friends, with backgrounds from medicine and microfinance, realized the lack of an affordable online healthcare service that caters to the primary care needs of the lower middle income and low income market.

One of them took in a software developer as partner and several video calls later, MediSure Plus was conceptualized with the mission of bringing affordable primary care to the homes of the target market, accessed through a web based app for ease and convenience.

Today

What We Offer

MediSure Plus is the first e-commerce store that offers affordable products and services focused on primary care for the family, personal health and well-being plus other items for the home. We continue to innovate and expand our services to better serve our community.
