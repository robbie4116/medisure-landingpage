About MSO Plus Konsulta EMR

MediSureOnline is a 5-year-old health IT company that developed its own electronic health record for its primary care facility. Through our actual experience as one of the pioneers of PhilHealth Konsulta, now known as YAKAP, we identified frontline needs and requirements and incorporated these learnings into our electronic health record, helping us pass the rigorous certification process of YAKAP PhilHealth.

Our system is compliant with Data Privacy laws and is built on FHIR (Fast Healthcare Interoperability Standards), designed to integrate seamlessly with modern healthcare systems. It provides all the core features required for YAKAP and more.

We are proud to share this user-friendly cloud-based app with your company so you can enjoy the seamless process that our MediSure clinic has long relied on, not only for PhilHealth collection but also for overall patient management.
